Dwight Kurt Schrute III

Assistant (to the) Regional Manager
Dwight is one of the highest-ranking salesmen at Dunder Mifflin paper distribution company. He has an active interest in the safety of his community and has served as a Lackawanna County volunteer sheriff's deputy, has a purple belt in Goju-Ryu karate, and is the senpai at his dojo. He is also trained in the art of survaillance.
Dwight also is a top contriutor to the economy of the community as the owner and proprietor at Schrute Farms, a bed and breakfast and beet plantation, as well as the owner of Scranton Business Park. Other entrepreneurial persuits of Dwight's include running an annual hay maze, opening a local cofee shop, and fitness center in the business park.
Dwight enjoys spending time in nature, science fiction, and is an avid collector of historical weaponry.
